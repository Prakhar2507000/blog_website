Places Must Visit

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in DEC. 2018

Visited Prithvi cafe on Independence Day. I won’t say it was crowded but full. Took approx 40 mins to get a table. We ordered Paneer kulcha, mixed paratha and veg biryani with 3 different drinks. Everything was served simultaneously while the drinks could have served quite early. 2ndly they served chicken biryani instead of veg, luckily we hadn’t started eating chicken biryani, mixed parantha were also replaced with paneer parantha. Taste wise, kulcha was good but charging extra for chhole is not acceptable. Paranthas were very poor in quality. Lot of dough was there and very less stuffing. Can’t say it’s a memorable experience. Travelled from Chembur to Prithvi considering I’ll have some memorable moments but disappointed. I’ll not recommend it to others. https://www.tripadvisor.com/ShowUserReviews-g304554-d1008673-r910517596-Prithvi_cafe-Mumbai_Maharashtra.html#

Jantar Mantar

I had visited in May 2020

It is considered one of the oldest astronomical observatories in the world, is a UNESCO heritage site, and hosts the largest sundial in the world which guarantees an accuracy of the order of a few seconds using only sunlight. A site of extraordinary interest. Make sure you have a good guide who is able to explain the wonders that the site contains.
